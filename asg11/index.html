My Ideal Dodgeball Team

Team Members


Vision Vanguards

Our dodgeball team is called Vision Vanguard, and we are built around speed, skill, and teamwork. Each member brings a special talent that helps the team stay strong in every match. We focus on quick movements, smart plays, and supporting each other on the court. With a mix of power, defense, strategy, and finishing strength, Vision Vanguard is ready to take on any challenge and play with confidence and unity.

Yoimiya

Yoimiya would be great for the team because she has very good aim and quick reactions. Since she works with fireworks, she is used to timing and accuracy. This helps her throw the ball fast and hit the target. She also has a friendly and positive attitude that helps keep everyone motivated. As captain she would bring energy and teamwork to every match.

Kazuha

Kazuha is calm and very good at moving quickly. He can dodge almost anything and stay focused even during intense moments. His light movements and air jumps make it hard for anyone to hit him. He would help the team by avoiding danger and keeping the game under control. His peaceful attitude also helps the team stay relaxed.

Itto

Itto is strong and confident which makes him perfect for the front line. He can block powerful throws and protect his teammates. He likes to challenge others which might make the other team aim at him instead. This helps take pressure off everyone else. When he throws the ball, his strength makes it very hard to catch.

Yelan

Yelan would be great because she is fast and very good at moving around without being noticed. She can slip past the other team and throw the ball before they even see her. Her quick thinking lets her make smart plays and surprise attacks. She can change direction easily which helps her avoid getting hit.

Xiao

Xiao is fast and precise which makes him the perfect player to finish a match. He can jump high and dodge several throws at once. When the game gets tough, he stays focused and makes strong winning shots. His speed and skill give the team a big advantage during important moments.
