Spider-man: The Human Spider

Overview

Spider-Man is a superhero in the American comic books published by Marvel Comics. He first appeared in the comic book Amazing Fantasy #15 in the June of 1962. Considered one of the most popular and commercially successful superheroes, he has been featured in comic books, television shows, films, video games, and in 2002, Spider-man turned into a movie with Tobie Maguire starring as Spiderman.

Spider-man is an intelligent but nerdy high school student, Peter Parker, who is bitten by a radioactive spider during a field trip, giving him superhuman powers.
