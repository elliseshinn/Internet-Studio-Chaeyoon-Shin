Presidential Candidate: Barbie

Barbie is a character created by Ruth Handler, manufactured by American toy and entertainment company Mattel and introduced on March 9, 1959. She is featured in many variations of series of shows and movies, most notably Barbie: Life in the Dreamhouse as well as other movies like Barbie: Fairy Secret. Barbie is known to be a caring and compassionate doll, with many jobs and is very qualified. Barbie's the eldest of four daughters, with her three younger sisters being Skipper, Stacie and Chelsea. For the most part, Barbie has a close relationship with all three of her sisters! She is also often talked about along with her counterpart doll Ken, who in the show is also her boyfriend.
